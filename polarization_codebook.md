# Polarization Questionnaire Scoring Codebook

| Score name | Construct | Description | Scale | Inputs | Formula | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `affpol_thermo` | Affective polarization (thermometer) | In-party warmth minus out-party warmth (0–100). | ~[-100, 100] | feelings to right_1, Q43_1, Political_affiliatio, Center/Other | thermo_inparty - thermo_outparty | Center participants treated as leaners using Center/Other. |
| `outparty_social_distance` | Social distance / avoidance | Mean of (reversed out-party friend comfort, reversed out-party neighbor comfort, out-party intermarriage upset). | [1, 4] approx | Right_Comfort, Left_Comfort, right_neighbors, left_neighbors, right_family, left_family | mean([5-friend_comfort_out, 5-neigh_comfort_out, marriage_upset_out]) | Comfort items reversed so higher = more rejection; upset is already higher=more. |
| `party_identity_strength` | Partisan identity strength | Mean of in-party identity importance, being in-party, in-party describes me, we-vs-they, and extremity strength. | mixed (approx 1–4.2) | Left_identity, right__identity, center/other_identit, left_being, right_being, center/other_being, left_describe, right_describe, center/othe_describe, we_or_they, Left_Extrem, Right_Extreme | mean([identity_in, being_in, describe_in, we_they(1–4), extremity_strength(1–2)]) |  Not rescaled. If you want a true 1–5 scale, rescale we_they and extremity before averaging. |
| `trait_bias` | Trait attribution bias | Combines positive-trait bias (pos_in - pos_out) and negative-trait bias (neg_out - neg_in). | ~[-4, 4] | right_table_1..8, left_table_1..8, Political_affiliatio, Center/Other | mean([pos_in_minus_out, neg_out_minus_in]) | Trait order per questionnaire table: patriotic,intelligent,honest,open-minded,generous,hypocritical,selfish,mean. |
| `trust_bias` | Trust bias | Trust in in-party minus trust in out-party. | [-4, 4] | right_trust, left_trust, Political_affiliatio, Center/Other | trust_inparty - trust_outparty |  |
| `thought_intensity_bias_unfav` | Thought intensity (unfavorable) | Unfavorable intensity out-party minus in-party (Hebrew 4-level intensity). | [-3, 3] | right_one_negative, left_one_negative, Political_affiliatio, Center/Other | unfav_outparty - unfav_inparty |  Direct intensity labels in this export (no separate presence+followup columns used). |
| `thought_intensity_bias_fav` | Thought intensity (favorable) | Favorable intensity in-party minus out-party (Hebrew 4-level intensity). | [-3, 3] | right_one_positive, left_one_positive, Political_affiliatio, Center/Other | fav_inparty - fav_outparty |  Direct intensity labels in this export. |
